Ennely

Another product I was engaged with, was Ennely.

Ennely is a video editor assistant that helps content creators put together a first cut of the final video, in minutes.

For Ennely to do its thing, the user just needs to add her footage and if needed, put the external audio along with the footage in what we call "synch buckets".

After that, Ennely detects all takes to pick the best.

Creating a scripted video has never been this fast and easy. Ennely is absolutely a revolutionizing tool for all content creators.
